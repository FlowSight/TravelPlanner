# Places to Visit in Pattaya, Thailand

## Beaches & Islands

- 1. Place Name: Pattaya Beach
	- Highlight: Central beach with water activities and nightlife nearby
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: Very busy, especially evenings
	- Timing: Morning to evening
	- Time to Cover: 1-3 hours

- 2. Place Name: Jomtien Beach
	- Highlight: Quieter, family-friendly beach stretch
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: Good for relaxed walks and cafés
	- Timing: Morning to sunset
	- Time to Cover: 2-4 hours

- 3. Place Name: Koh Larn (Coral Island)
	- Highlight: Clearer water, beach hopping, snorkeling
	- Fee: Ferry ~30-50 THB one way
	- Notes: Day trip from Bali Hai Pier
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: Half day to full day

---

## Viewpoints & Scenic Spots

- 4. Place Name: Pattaya Viewpoint (Khao Pattaya View Point)
	- Highlight: Panoramic city and bay views
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: Great photo spot
	- Timing: Sunset
	- Time to Cover: 30-60 minutes

- 5. Place Name: Pratumnak Hill
	- Highlight: Coastal roads, viewpoints, quieter vibe
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: Combine with nearby cafés
	- Timing: Late afternoon
	- Time to Cover: 1-2 hours

---

## Cultural & Religious Sites

- 6. Place Name: Sanctuary of Truth
	- Highlight: Massive hand-carved wooden temple-like structure
	- Fee: ~500 THB
	- Notes: Unique architecture and craftsmanship
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 1.5-2.5 hours

- 7. Place Name: Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai)
	- Highlight: Large golden Buddha and hilltop views
	- Fee: Free (donations)
	- Notes: Dress respectfully
	- Timing: Morning or late afternoon
	- Time to Cover: 45-90 minutes

- 8. Place Name: Wat Yansangwararam
	- Highlight: Peaceful temple complex with gardens
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: Good for a calm visit
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 1-2 hours

---

## Gardens & Family Attractions

- 9. Place Name: Nong Nooch Tropical Garden
	- Highlight: Cultural shows, themed gardens, dinosaur valley
	- Fee: ~500-600 THB
	- Notes: Large area; allow half/full day
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 4-8 hours

- 10. Place Name: Ramayana Water Park
	- Highlight: One of Thailand’s largest water parks
	- Fee: ~1,000+ THB (varies)
	- Notes: Check promotions online
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 4-6 hours

- 11. Place Name: Underwater World Pattaya
	- Highlight: Walk-through aquarium tunnel
	- Fee: ~500 THB
	- Notes: Family-friendly indoor option
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 1-2 hours

---

## Markets & Shopping

- 12. Place Name: Pattaya Floating Market
	- Highlight: Cultural/shopping market
	- Fee: Entry varies
	- Notes: Souvenirs, snacks, boat rides
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 2-3 hours

- 13. Place Name: Thepprasit Night Market
	- Highlight: Weekend night market
	- Fee: Free entry
	- Notes: Street food, clothes, local items
	- Timing: Fri-Sun evening
	- Time to Cover: 2-3 hours

- 14. Place Name: Central Pattaya (Festival)
	- Highlight: Shopping mall
	- Fee: Free entry
	- Notes: Shopping, dining, cinema
	- Timing: Anytime
	- Time to Cover: 2-4 hours

## Day Trip Ideas

- 17. Place Name: Koh Larn
	- Highlight: Beaches, snorkeling, island vibe
	- Fee: Ferry/speedboat fare applies
	- Notes: Distance/time is 30-45 min by ferry/speedboat
	- Timing: Day trip (morning start recommended)
	- Time to Cover: 5-8 hours

- 18. Place Name: Khao Chi Chan (Buddha Mountain)
	- Highlight: Giant Buddha image on cliff
	- Fee: Free
	- Notes: About 30-40 min from Pattaya
	- Timing: Morning or late afternoon
	- Time to Cover: 1-2 hours

- 19. Place Name: Silverlake Vineyard area
	- Highlight: Scenic countryside views
	- Fee: Varies by activity
	- Notes: Nearby Khao Chi Chan
	- Timing: Daytime
	- Time to Cover: 1-2 hours
