# Thailand Trip Itinerary — Bangkok & Pattaya

**Route:** Bangalore → Bangkok → Pattaya → Bangkok → Bangalore
**Duration:** 7 Days / 6 Nights (3N Bangkok + 2N Pattaya + 1N Bangkok)

> Why this order? International flights land at Bangkok (Suvarnabhumi).
> Explore Bangkok first, then head to Pattaya (~2-2.5 hrs by road).
> Return to Bangkok on the last evening for the departure flight next day.

---

## Trip Overview

- Days in Bangkok: 3 full days (Day 1 evening + Day 2 + Day 3 + Day 4 morning)
- Days in Pattaya: 2 full days (Day 4 evening + Day 5 + Day 6 afternoon)
- Travel between cities: ~2-2.5 hours each way (bus, minivan, or Grab)
- Pace: Relaxed — rest/break built in after every 2-3 hours of activity

---

## Day 1 — Arrival in Bangkok

**Theme:** Land, settle in, easy evening stroll

### Afternoon
- Arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK)
- Immigration, collect luggage
- Travel to hotel (BTS/Grab — ~45-60 min to central Bangkok)
- Check in, freshen up, rest for 1-2 hours

### Evening (~5 PM onward)
- **#11 Khao San Road** — Easy stroll through the backpacker street
  - Street snacks, cold drinks, people-watching
  - Time: 2-3 hours
- Dinner at Khao San Road itself (pad thai, mango sticky rice from street vendors)
- Return to hotel, early rest (adjust to timezone)

> Tip: Download Grab app & buy a local SIM at the airport. Carry an umbrella.

---

## Day 2 — Temples & the River

**Theme:** Old Bangkok — temples, river, night market

### Morning (8:30 AM start)
- **#1 Wat Phra Kaew + #4 Grand Palace** — Same complex, one ticket (~500 THB)
  - Thailand's most sacred temple + former royal residence
  - Dress code: no shorts, no sleeveless, no revealing clothes for women
  - Time: ~2.5-3 hours

### Mid-morning Break (~11:30 AM)
- Rest at a nearby café, hydrate, cool down
- ~30-45 minutes

### Late Morning / Early Afternoon (12:15 PM)
- **#2 Wat Pho** — 5-minute walk from Grand Palace
  - 46m gold-plated Reclining Buddha
  - Optional: get a Thai massage at the school on site (~1 hour)
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours

### Lunch & Rest (1:30 PM - 3:30 PM)
- Lunch at a nearby restaurant (Tha Tien area has great local food)
- Return to hotel for afternoon rest / nap (~1.5-2 hours)

### Late Afternoon (4:00 PM)
- **#3 Wat Arun** — Short ferry ride across the Chao Phraya River from Wat Pho
  - Climb the colorful porcelain spire, enjoy river views
  - Best at sunset
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours

### Evening (6:00 PM)
- **#7 Asiatique The Riverfront** — Night market along the river
  - Free ferry from Saphan Taksin BTS
  - Dinner + shopping + entertainment
  - Time: 2-3 hours

> Day total: ~8 hours of activity with two proper breaks in between.

---

## Day 3 — Culture, Shopping & City Views

**Theme:** Art, malls, skyline at sunset, street food at night

### Morning (9:30 AM)
- **#5 Jim Thompson House** — Silk museum in beautiful teak houses
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours
  - Fee: ~200 THB

### Late Morning (11:00 AM)
- Walk/BTS to **#9 Siam Paragon / CentralWorld**
  - Browse the upscale malls, excellent food courts for lunch
  - Air-conditioned break from the heat
  - Time: 2-3 hours (including lunch)

### Afternoon Rest (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)
- Return to hotel or relax at **#13 Lumpini Park** for a gentle walk
  - Monitor lizards, paddle boats, shaded paths
  - Time: 1 hour if you visit

### Late Afternoon (4:30 PM)
- **#15 Mahanakhon SkyWalk** — Thailand's highest observation deck (314m)
  - Glass floor, 360° views — time it for sunset
  - Fee: ~800-1000 THB
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours

### Evening (6:30 PM)
- **#10 Yaowarat (Chinatown)** — Bangkok's best street food area
  - Must-try: Pad Thai, roasted duck, mango sticky rice, dim sum
  - Time: 2-3 hours for walk + dinner

### Night (optional, 9:30 PM)
- **#17 Sky Bar (Lebua)** or **#18 Vertigo & Moon Bar** — Rooftop drinks with city views
  - Pay for drinks only, no entry fee
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours
  - Skip if tired — completely optional

> Day total: ~8-9 hours of activity with afternoon break built in.
> Tip: If your Day 2 or 3 falls on a Sat/Sun, swap the morning plan with **#6 Chatuchak Weekend Market** (Sat-Sun 9 AM - 6 PM, 15,000+ stalls, 3-5 hours). It's worth rearranging for.

## Day 4 — Last Bangkok Stop + Travel to Pattaya

**Theme:** Morning shopping, afternoon travel, Pattaya evening

### Morning (9:00 AM)
- **#8 MBK Center** — Great for electronics, souvenirs, phone accessories
  - Time: 1.5-2 hours
  - OR revisit Siam area / pick up anything you missed

### Late Morning (11:30 AM)
- Check out of Bangkok hotel
- Grab lunch near hotel or at a mall food court

### Afternoon (12:30 PM - 3:00 PM)
- Travel to Pattaya
  - Option 1: Bus from Ekkamai Eastern Bus Terminal (~120-150 THB, ~2.5 hours)
  - Option 2: Private car or Grab (~1,500-2,500 THB, ~2 hours)
  - Option 3: Minivan from Victory Monument or Mo Chit

### Afternoon (3:00 PM - 4:30 PM)
- Arrive in Pattaya, check into hotel, freshen up, rest

### Late Afternoon (5:00 PM)
- **Pattaya #1 Pattaya Beach** — Evening stroll along the central beach
  - Time: 1-1.5 hours

### Evening (7:00 PM)
- **Pattaya #15 Walking Street** — Pattaya's famous nightlife strip
  - Walk through, dinner at a nearby restaurant
  - Time: 1.5-2 hours
- Return to hotel

---

## Day 5 — Pattaya Sightseeing

**Theme:** Temples, culture, viewpoint at sunset

### Morning (9:00 AM)
- **Pattaya #6 Sanctuary of Truth** — Massive hand-carved wooden temple
  - Unique architecture, one of Pattaya's top attractions
  - Fee: ~500 THB
  - Time: 1.5-2.5 hours

### Mid-morning Break (11:30 AM)
- Café or juice break nearby, cool down

### Late Morning (12:00 PM)
- **Pattaya #7 Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai)** — Hilltop golden Buddha
  - Free (donations), dress respectfully
  - Time: 45-90 minutes

### Lunch & Rest (1:30 PM - 3:30 PM)
- Lunch at a local restaurant
- Return to hotel for afternoon rest

### Afternoon (3:30 PM)
- **Pattaya #12 Pattaya Floating Market** — Souvenirs, snacks, boat rides
  - Time: 2-3 hours

### Sunset (5:30 PM)
- **Pattaya #4 Pattaya Viewpoint (Khao Pattaya View Point)** — Panoramic bay views
  - Best at sunset, great photo spot
  - Time: 30-60 minutes

### Evening (7:00 PM)
- **Pattaya #16 Terminal 21 Pattaya** — Themed mall with excellent food court
  - Dinner + light shopping
  - Time: 1.5-2 hours

> Day total: ~8 hours of activity, well spaced with breaks.

---

## Day 6 — Beach / Island Day + Return to Bangkok

**Theme:** Relaxed beach morning, travel back in the evening

### Option A: Koh Larn Island Trip (recommended if weather is good)
- **Morning (8:30 AM):** Ferry from Bali Hai Pier to **Pattaya #3 Koh Larn**
  - Ferry: ~30-50 THB one way, ~45 min
  - Snorkeling, beach hopping, clear water
  - Lunch on the island
  - Time: 5-6 hours
- **Return by 3:00 PM**

### Option B: Beach + Countryside (if you prefer land-based)
- **Morning:** **Pattaya #2 Jomtien Beach** — Quiet beach, relaxed walk, cafés (2-3 hours)
- **Midday break & lunch**
- **Early afternoon:** **Pattaya #18 Khao Chi Chan (Buddha Mountain)** + **Pattaya #19 Silverlake Vineyard** — 30-40 min from Pattaya, scenic drive (2-3 hours combined)

### Late Afternoon (4:00 PM)
- Check out of Pattaya hotel
- Travel back to Bangkok (~2-2.5 hours)

### Evening (6:30 PM)
- Arrive Bangkok, check into airport-area hotel
- Light dinner nearby, pack, early rest

---

## Day 7 — Departure

**Theme:** Last bits + fly home

### Morning
- Sleep in, or quick visit to a nearby 7-Eleven for last-minute snacks/souvenirs
- Breakfast at hotel

### Midday / Afternoon
- Head to Suvarnabhumi Airport (~30 min from airport-area hotel)
- Departure formalities
- Fly back to Bangalore

## Packing / General Reminders

- Carry an umbrella (rain can be sudden)
- Modest clothing for all temples (cover shoulders and knees)
- Sunscreen, hat, water bottle
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Local SIM card from airport (TrueMove or AIS)
- Grab app for rides
- 7-Eleven is everywhere — cheap snacks, water, essentials
- Keep valuables secure in crowded areas
- Best months: November to February (cool and dry)
